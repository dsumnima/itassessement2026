Who We Are

A Legacy of Hospitality

Founded with a vision to offer the finest blend of Nepali warmth and modern luxury, Sumi's Resort has been welcoming guests from around the world for over a decade.

Our resort is more than just a place to stay — it is a destination where you can reconnect with nature, unwind in comfort, and create memories that last a lifetime.

Situated in the lush surroundings of Nepal, every element of our property has been thoughtfully designed to harmonize with the natural environment while providing every modern comfort.
